<objective>
Promote `voss-tui`'s proven `HttpClient` (net.rs) and handshake parse (server.rs) into the library-grade `VossClient` REST surface and `Handshake` auth helper. This delivers VSDK-RS-02 (typed REST client, typed errors) and VSDK-RS-05 (handshake + bearer helpers), the foundation the stream (Wave 3), supervisor (Wave 3), and integration suite (Wave 4) all consume.

Purpose: Every endpoint becomes a typed, bearer-authenticated method returning `Result<T, VossError>` — no `anyhow` leaks into the public API. The handshake helper owns the local-server token concern (distinct from `voss-auth` provider creds and `voss-bridge` JSON-RPC).

Output: `crates/voss-sdk/src/client.rs` (VossClient) + `crates/voss-sdk/src/auth.rs` (Handshake), both re-exported from lib.rs; `cargo test -p voss-sdk --lib` green.
</objective>
